# ClearSpend App Store Optimization

Last updated: 2026-05-19

## Default Product Page

Use this as the broad App Store page for people searching for a budget, spending, expense, or receipt tracker.

Name:
ClearSpend

Subtitle:
Daily spending clarity

Subtitle alternates:
- Safe daily budget tracker
- Budget, receipts, goals
- Daily budget clarity

Promotional text:
Know what you can safely spend today. ClearSpend turns your real monthly budget into a daily number, with receipt scanning, goals, subscriptions, and AI insights.

Keywords:
budget,expense,spending,receipt,scanner,subscriptions,goals,currency,finance,AI,insights

Description:
ClearSpend helps you understand what you can safely spend today.

Instead of starting with a vague monthly budget, ClearSpend turns your income, essential bills, savings goal, payday, and recent spending into one daily number you can actually use.

Use it to:
- See your Safe to Spend Today number
- Track income, expenses, balance, and budget health
- Scan receipts and review itemized expenses before saving
- Search and filter your transaction history
- Import and export CSV data
- Track savings goals and App Store subscriptions
- Log supported foreign-currency purchases
- Generate optional AI daily, weekly, monthly, and forecast insights
- Personalize themes, color mode, font style, currency, and language

ClearSpend is built for people who want a calm money check, not another spreadsheet. Open the app, check your number, log what changed, and keep moving.

Free includes the daily dashboard, goals, subscriptions, CSV tools, multi-currency tracking, limited AI check-ins, and limited receipt scans.

ClearSpend Pro adds more AI analyses, forecasts, custom categories, visual charts, auto analysis controls, and unlimited receipt scanning.

## Localization Notes

ClearSpend supports English, Japanese, and Simplified Chinese in-app. Localize the App Store description, keywords, screenshots, and app previews for each market you want to target.

Do not claim:
- Fully offline
- No data ever leaves the device
- Background auto analysis is already running
- Voice input is available in the visible app flow
- Bank connection or automatic bank import

Safer claims:
- Local-first budget setup
- Use guest mode without creating an account
- AI insights when you choose to generate them
- Receipt scanning uses OCR and AI extraction
- Export your data any time
